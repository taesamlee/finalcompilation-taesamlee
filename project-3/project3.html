Summer 2019

Internship Journal

For Summer 2019, I joined La Victoria Lab’s (LVL) Digital Transformation and FEST group as a Design Intern. LVL is Intercorp’s design and innovation studio staffed by an interdisciplinary Intercorp and IDEO team. The lab initiates projects that create rapid growth and transformation within a broad portfolio of businesses, including banking, retail ventures, healthcare services, and education.

In the Digital Transformation team I helped envision the new ways of work inside Intercorp. As part of this interdisciplinary team, I was working as a UX researcher identifying points of friction at the workplace, specifically with early stage digital product teams.

I had the incredible opportunity to design and launch a responsive website for the digital product fair inside Intercorp’s yearly innovation festival. I welcome you to take a look at the live website.
